Olinda Collection

L'Occitane au Brésil inspired in the joyful city of Olinda to create this fruity and floral fragrance.

Olinda hosts one of the world's most famous carnivals and is listed as a World Heritage Site by UNESCO due to its XVI and XVII-century buildings.
